Safe Foods for Dogs

A comprehensive guide to feeding your dog healthy human foods

Quick Reference Guide

Safe Fruits

  • Apples (no seeds)
  • Bananas
  • Blueberries
  • Watermelon (no seeds)
  • Cantaloupe
  • Pears (seedless)

Safe Vegetables

  • Carrots
  • Green beans
  • Sweet potatoes (cooked)
  • Pumpkin (plain)
  • Broccoli (moderate)
  • Cucumbers

Safe Proteins

  • Chicken (plain)
  • Lean beef
  • Turkey (unseasoned)
  • Fish (cooked)
  • Eggs (cooked)

Important Guidelines

✓ Always introduce new foods gradually

✓ Remove seeds, pits, and rinds

✓ Serve in appropriate portions

✓ Watch for allergic reactions

✓ Consult your vet before major diet changes

Foods to Avoid

  • Chocolate
  • Grapes and raisins
  • Onions and garlic
  • Macadamia nuts
  • Avocado
  • Xylitol (artificial sweetener)
  • Raw/undercooked meat
  • Salty snacks and candy
  • Caffeine
  • Alcohol

Preparation Tips

Fruits & Vegetables

  • Wash thoroughly
  • Cut into small, manageable pieces
  • Remove seeds and pits
  • Serve raw or lightly cooked

Proteins

  • Cook thoroughly
  • Avoid seasonings
  • Remove bones
  • Serve at room temperature
